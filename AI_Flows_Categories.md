# AI Pipeline Categories (Exhaustive List)
I've expanded your initial list by cross-referencing industry standards from Hugging Face, research papers, and creative AI platforms. You can trim this down to what fits the "Vault-Flows" philosophy.

## 1. Visual & Graphics (Creative Focus)
Latent Image Diffusion: T2I, I2I, ControlNet workflows, LoRA merging.
Video Editing & Synthesis: Frame interpolation, temporal consistency, rotoscoping, upscaling.
3D Modeling & Mesh Manipulation: NeRF/Gaussian Splatting, rigging automation, texture generation.
USD Creation: Scene orchestration, layer management, NVIDIA Omniverse connectors.
Visual Storytelling: Multi-frame consistency (IP-Adapter), shot-to-shot logic, script-to-comic.
Motion Capture & Pose: Human pose transfer, hand-tracking refinement, facial re-targeting.
Neural Rendering: Light estimation, HDRI generation, relighting pipelines.


## 2. Audio & Spoken Language
STT & TTS: Transcription, cloned voices, emotional prosody control.
Audio Manipulation: Stem separation (vocal/instrumental), noise reduction, foley generation.
Music Synthesis: MIDI generation, style transfer, arrangement automation.
Audio-to-Video: Lipsync automation (LivePortrait/Talker), audio-reactive visuals.


## 3. Natural Language & Intelligence
Text Improvement: Style transfer (e.g., "Professional" to "Street"), recursive editing, grammar-to-mood.
RAG & Knowledge Flows: Vector DB ingestion, semantic search pipelines, synthetic data generation.
Multimodal VQA: Detailed image/video interrogation (captioning + logic).
Translation & Localization: Cultural adaptation, lip-sync-aligned translation.
Agentic Planning: Goal decomposition, tool-use selection, self-correction loops.


## 4. Utility & Structural (The "Plumbing")
Data Transformation: OCR to JSON, CSV normalization, bulk metadata tagging.
Quality Control: NSFW filtering, perceptual hash comparison, aesthetic scoring.
Coding & Infrastructure: Unit test generation, documentation scrapers, CI/CD trigger flows.
Document Intelligence: Form extraction, layout-aware PDF analysis.


## 5. Specialized & Niche
Medical/Legal: Anonymization, legal clause comparison, medical imaging segmentation.
Game Dev: Procedural level generation, NPC dialogue trees, skybox generation.
Social & Marketing: Ad-copy-to-image variants, trend analysis-to-post.
